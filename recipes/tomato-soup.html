Creamy Roasted Red Pepper Tomato Soup

Description

A recipe for a delicious and warming soup, just in time for the first frost, especially if we season it spicy - with a lot of pepper and hot paprika. The soup is great for dinner or lunch, it is also worth serving it with toast.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium red peppers
  • 2 cans of canned tomatoes
  • 1 medium onion
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic
  • 4-5 sun-dried tomatoes
  • 1 mozzarella ball
  • 1 tsp of sweet and hot paprika
  • a bit of salt and optionally sugar

Instruction

  1. Wash the peppers, put them whole on a baking tray lined with baking paper and bake at a temperature of approx. 200Â° C for approx. 25 minutes. Once the peppers are roasted, peel the skin off. If the skin comes off with difficulty - you should put the still hot pepper into a foil bag, tie it and leave it for about 5-10 minutes, after this time there should be no problem with peeling.

  2. While the peppers are baking, chop the dried tomatoes, put them in a pot together with the chopped onion and garlic, glaze, add the tomatoes and cook, season to taste.

  3. Add the roasted pepper, optionally set aside some and cut into cubes, leave for decoration.

  4. Blend the soup into a cream, bring to a boil. Serve in bowls and top with piece of mozzarella cheese, croutons, sliced peppers and basil leaf for garnish.
